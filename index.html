What we do

Based in the heart of Gloucestershire, we rescue and re-home hundreds of unwanted and abandoned cats, dogs and small animals every year at a cost of around £750,000.

Thanks to the generosity of the public we can continue to look after the animals in our care. We receive no Government or Lottery funding.
